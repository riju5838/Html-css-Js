india, the land of many colours, has sailed some rough waters till it gained freedom,

thanks to the freedom fighters who envisioned the country as free land and fought for our rights without caring for their lives.

Among them was Subhash Chandra Bose, a great nationalist and prominent freedom fighter who actively participated in the Non-Cooperation Movement. His contributions are vast, and therefore, a write-up on Subhash Chandra Bose is commonly assigned to kids. In this article, we talk about how to write short and long essays on the topic. Considering the significance of Bose as a freedom fighter, it is important to get the facts and the narrative right. We will introduce important facts children need to remember about him, and provide some tips on using them in an essay for classes 1, 2 and 3!
